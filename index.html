My Minecraft Mods

Strippable Stone:

If you ever have any excess polished blocks, stone bricks, or normal stone, that you want to turn back into it's old block, you can strip it to do so super simply!



Bloody Skies:

This mod aims to add in several different items, blocks and other features concerning sacrifice, and empowering yourself...



Mob-Crackers:

This mod adds a 33% chance for all types of monsters to drop Mob-Crackers, popping them open will have a chance to drop Cookies, Jokes, and Paper Crowns
